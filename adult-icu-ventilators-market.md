<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Non-Invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adult ICU ventilators market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive ventilation. Invasive ventilation involves the use of endotracheal tubes or tracheostomy to deliver air directly into the lungs, suitable for critically ill patients requiring comprehensive respiratory support. Non-invasive ventilation employs facial masks or nasal interfaces, providing oxygen support without the need for intubation, often used for patients with COPD or acute respiratory distress. Both types are essential for managing respiratory failure and improving patient outcomes in intensive care settings.</p></p>
