<p><p>The agriculture pump set market primarily features two main types: centrifugal pumps and displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to move water, making them ideal for high-flow applications like irrigation. They are efficient and widely used in various farming scenarios. Displacement pumps, on the other hand, operate by trapping a fixed amount of water and forcing it out, which makes them suitable for tasks requiring precise control of flow rates. Both types cater to diverse agricultural needs, enhancing productivity.</p></p>
