<p><strong>The Blind Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FF</li><li>RF</li><li>MFM</li><li>TG</li><li>RJ</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blind flanges market features several types, each serving distinct applications. Flat Face (FF) blind flanges provide a smooth surface for even sealing. Raised Face (RF) flanges offer a slight elevation for better sealing pressure. Male-Female (MFM) flanges ensure a snug fit through interlocking designs. Tongue and Groove (TG) flanges enhance alignment and sealing with a tongue and groove joint. Finally, Ring Joint (RJ) flanges utilize a metallic ring for effective sealing, making them ideal for high-pressure environments.</p></p>
