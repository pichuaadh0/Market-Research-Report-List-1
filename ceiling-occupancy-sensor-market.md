<p><p>The ceiling occupancy sensor market includes various types of technologies for detecting human presence. Ultrasonic monitoring emits sound waves to detect motion, effectively covering larger areas and identifying subtle movements, suitable for complex spaces. Other market segments may include dual-technology sensors that combine both PIR and ultrasonic methods for enhanced accuracy and reliability in occupancy detection.</p></p>
