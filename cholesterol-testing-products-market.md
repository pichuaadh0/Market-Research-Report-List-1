<p><strong>The Cholesterol Testing Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Cholesterol Test</li><li>High-Density Lipoprotein (HDL) Cholesterol Test</li><li>Low-Density Lipoprotein (LDL) Cholesterol Test</li><li>Triglycerides/Very-Low-Density Lipoprotein (VLDL) Cholesterol Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cholesterol testing products market is segmented into several key types. The total cholesterol test measures overall cholesterol levels, providing a broad overview of heart health. The HDL cholesterol test focuses on measuring the “good” cholesterol, which helps remove other forms of cholesterol from the bloodstream. The LDL cholesterol test assesses the “bad” cholesterol that can lead to plaque buildup in arteries. Lastly, the triglycerides/VLDL cholesterol test evaluates triglyceride levels and very-low-density lipoprotein, both important for cardiovascular risk assessment.</p></p>
