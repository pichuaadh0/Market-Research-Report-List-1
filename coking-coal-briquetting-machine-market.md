<p><strong>The Coking Coal Briquetting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coking coal briquetting machine market is segmented into two main types: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical briquetting machines utilize a system of rollers to compress and shape coal fines into briquettes, offering a cost-effective solution for medium to large-scale production. In contrast, hydraulic briquetting machines employ hydraulic pressure to achieve higher density and uniformity in briquettes, making them ideal for specialized applications and smaller production scales. Both types cater to different industry needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
