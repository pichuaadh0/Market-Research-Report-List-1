<p><p>The consumer drones market can be categorized into three main types: multi-rotor, fixed-wing, and single rotor. Multi-rotor drones, commonly used for aerial photography and recreational flying, feature multiple rotors for enhanced stability and maneuverability. Fixed-wing drones are designed for longer flight durations and cover more extensive areas, suited for surveying and mapping. Single rotor drones combine aspects of both, offering longer flight times than multi-rotors but generally require more skill to operate, making them popular for specific applications like agricultural monitoring.</p></p>
