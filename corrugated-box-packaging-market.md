<p><p>The corrugated box packaging market comprises three main types: single, double, and triple corrugated boxes. Single corrugated boxes consist of a single layer of fluted material sandwiched between two liners, suitable for lightweight products. Double corrugated boxes have two layers of fluted material, offering greater strength for medium-weight items and enhanced protection. Triple corrugated boxes feature three layers of fluted material, ideal for heavy or fragile goods requiring maximum durability and cushioning during transportation and storage.</p></p>
