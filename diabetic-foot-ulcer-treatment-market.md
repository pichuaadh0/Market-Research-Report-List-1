<p><p>The diabetic foot ulcer treatment market is categorized into three main types: neuropathic ulcers, ischemic ulcers, and neuro-ischemic ulcers. Neuropathic ulcers result from nerve damage, leading to loss of sensation and injury due to pressure or trauma. Ischemic ulcers occur from reduced blood flow, often linked to peripheral artery disease, causing tissue damage. Neuro-ischemic ulcers exhibit characteristics of both, with nerve damage and compromised blood circulation, presenting significant challenges in healing. Each type requires specialized treatment approaches to promote recovery.</p></p>
