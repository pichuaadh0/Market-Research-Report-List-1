<p><ul><li>Without Water Trapper</li><li>With One Water Trapper</li><li>With Two Water Trapper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disposable Ventilator Circuit market is segmented into three types based on water trap configurations. "Without Water Trapper" circuits allow for unobstructed airflow, making them suitable for specific applications but may require more frequent circuit changes. "With One Water Trapper" circuits feature a single trap to collect condensation, enhancing performance and reducing maintenance but still allowing some moisture to pass. "With Two Water Trapper" circuits provide maximum moisture control, ideal for critical care settings where tube condensation can impact patient safety.</p></p>
