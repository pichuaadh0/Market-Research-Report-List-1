<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) systems primarily fall into two market types: DASI (Distributed Acoustic Sensor Interface) and DASP (Distributed Acoustic Sensor Processing). DASI focuses on the hardware interfaces and integration of sensors into various applications, providing the foundational infrastructure for data collection. In contrast, DASP centers on data analysis, processing algorithms, and applications, enabling advanced interpretation and decision-making based on the acoustic data collected. Together, they drive innovation across sectors like oil and gas, infrastructure monitoring, and security.</p></p>
