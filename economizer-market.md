<p><strong>The Economizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Economizers</li><li>Air-side Economizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The economizer market is categorized into fluid economizers and air-side economizers, both focused on enhancing energy efficiency in HVAC systems. Fluid economizers utilize chilled water or other fluids to cool buildings by leveraging outdoor temperatures, reducing refrigeration load. Air-side economizers, on the other hand, take advantage of outdoor air to cool spaces directly, minimizing reliance on mechanical cooling. Both types aim to lower energy consumption and operational costs while improving indoor air quality and comfort in commercial and industrial facilities.</p></p>
