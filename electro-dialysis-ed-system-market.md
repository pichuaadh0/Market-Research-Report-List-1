<p><ul><li>Continuous Electro Dialysis</li><li>Batch Electro Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro Dialysis (ED) systems are classified into two main types: Continuous Electro Dialysis and Batch Electro Dialysis. Continuous ED systems operate continuously, allowing for a steady flow of feed solution through the anionic and cationic membranes, enhancing efficiency for large-scale applications. In contrast, Batch ED systems process solutions in discrete batches, making them suitable for varied or small-scale operations. Both types utilize electric current to separate ions, providing effective desalination and concentration solutions for various industries.</p></p>
