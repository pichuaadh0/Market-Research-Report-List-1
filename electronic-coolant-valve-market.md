<p><p>The electronic coolant valve market is segmented primarily into two types: two-way valves and three-way valves, along with other specialized varieties. Two-way valves control the flow of coolant in a single direction, making them ideal for straightforward applications. In contrast, three-way valves can redirect or mix coolant flows, providing greater flexibility in temperature and flow regulation. Other valves may include variable or multi-port designs, catering to specific industrial needs and enhancing system efficiency in automotive and HVAC applications.</p></p>
