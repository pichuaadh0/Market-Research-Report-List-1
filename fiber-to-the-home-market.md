<p><strong>The Fiber to the Home Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber to the Home (FTTH) market is primarily divided into single-mode and multimode fiber types. Single-mode fiber utilizes a single light path, enabling long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making it ideal for large-scale networks. In contrast, multimode fiber supports multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distances and higher data transmission rates in localized environments, such as commercial buildings. Each type addresses specific needs in the growing demand for high-speed internet connectivity and reliable telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
