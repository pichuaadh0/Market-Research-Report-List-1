<p><p>The fire protection piping system market includes three primary types: seamless steel pipes, welded steel tubes, and CPVC (Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride) pipes. Seamless steel pipes are known for their strength and resistance to high pressures, making them ideal for high-risk environments. Welded steel tubes provide a cost-effective solution with flexibility in design, suitable for various applications. CPVC offers excellent corrosion resistance and is lightweight, making it suitable for residential and commercial fire protection systems. Each type caters to specific needs in fire safety installations.</p></p>
