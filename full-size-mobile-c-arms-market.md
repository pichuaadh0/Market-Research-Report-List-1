<p><p>The full-size mobile C-arms market is divided into two main types based on fluoroscopy techniques: Full-size Mobile C-arms with Pulsed Fluoroscopy and those with Continuous Fluoroscopy. Pulsed fluoroscopy offers intermittent imaging, which reduces radiation exposure and improves image quality by minimizing motion blur, making it ideal for specific procedures. In contrast, continuous fluoroscopy provides a steady stream of images, useful for real-time monitoring during surgeries and complex interventions, ensuring comprehensive visibility for medical professionals throughout operations.</p></p>
