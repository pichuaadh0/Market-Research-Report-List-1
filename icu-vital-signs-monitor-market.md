Mid-acuity monitors cater to patients with moderate conditions, providing essential vital sign tracking without extensive capabilities. Low-acuity monitors are suited for stable patients in less critical environments, focusing on basic vital signs and often featuring user-friendly interfaces for ease of use in routine monitoring.</p></p>
