<p><p>Industrial static eliminators are categorized based on their design and application. Bar type eliminators utilize charged bars to neutralize static across surfaces, ideal for long, narrow areas. Nozzle type devices direct ionized air to eliminate static on specific points or surfaces, enhancing precision. Fan type models disperse ionized air over larger areas, suitable for high-speed operations. Gun type eliminators are handheld, offering flexibility for targeted applications. Other types may include specialty devices tailored for unique industrial needs, expanding their usability across various sectors.</p></p>
