<p><strong>The Laboratory Gas Scrubbers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jet & Venturi Scrubbers</li><li>Orifice Scrubbers</li><li>Packed Bed Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory gas scrubbers market includes various types designed for efficient gas processing. Jet and Venturi scrubbers utilize high-velocity jets to mix gas with scrubbing liquid, effectively removing particulates and pollutants. Orifice scrubbers employ holes to atomize the liquid, enhancing gas absorption. Packed bed scrubbers feature a column filled with packing material, maximizing gas-liquid contact for pollutant capture. Each type serves specific applications based on efficiency, space requirements, and the characteristics of the gases being treated.</p></p>
