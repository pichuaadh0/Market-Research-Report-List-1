BEVs operate solely on electric power from onboard batteries, offering zero tailpipe emissions and longer driving ranges as battery technology improves. PEVs, on the other hand, combine electric power with a conventional internal combustion engine, allowing for extended range through gasoline backup. Both categories respond to consumer demand for sustainable transportation and are crucial in reducing carbon footprints in the automotive sector.</p></p>
