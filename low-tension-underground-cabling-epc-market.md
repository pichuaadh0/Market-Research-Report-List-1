<p><p>The Low Tension Underground Cabling EPC market comprises three primary types of insulation materials: PVC, XLPE, and Rubber. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and widely used for low-voltage applications, offering good mechanical protection. XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) provides superior thermal resistance and electrical performance, making it suitable for higher voltage and rigorous environment applications. Rubber cables offer flexibility and resilience, ideal for installations requiring adaptability to movement and varying conditions. Each type serves distinct operational needs in underground cabling infrastructure.</p></p>
