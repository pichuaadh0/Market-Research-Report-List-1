<p><p>The manual rivet gun market primarily includes two types: ring groove rivet guns and core pulling rivet guns. Ring groove rivet guns are designed for creating rivets with a specific groove shape, ensuring secure fastening in applications such as plumbing and construction. Core pulling rivet guns, on the other hand, are used for extracting mandrels from blind rivets, making them ideal for situations where access is limited. Both tools enhance efficiency and precision in various riveting tasks across multiple industries.</p></p>
