<p><p>The Meat Moisture Analyzer market primarily features DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared) technology. DEXA technology provides precise moisture content analysis by utilizing dual-energy X-ray beams, ensuring accurate readings for quality control in meat processing. In contrast, NIR technology employs infrared light to measure moisture content quickly and non-destructively, offering rapid results suitable for real-time monitoring. Both technologies enhance product quality, streamline processing, and ensure compliance with industry standards in meat production.</p></p>
