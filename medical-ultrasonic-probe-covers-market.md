<p><p>The medical ultrasonic probe covers market is categorized into latex-free and latex probe covers. Latex-free probe covers are designed for patients with latex allergies, providing a safer alternative while maintaining hygiene and preventing cross-contamination. These covers are often made from materials such as polyethylene or other synthetic options. In contrast, latex probe covers are made from natural rubber latex, offering elasticity and effective barrier protection. While they may be cost-effective, they pose a risk for allergic reactions in sensitive individuals.</p></p>
