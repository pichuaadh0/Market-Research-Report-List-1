<p><p>The mercerised cotton market comprises three key segments: yarn mercerization, knit mercerization, and cloth mercerization. Yarn mercerization involves treating cotton yarn to enhance its strength, sheen, and dye affinity. Knit mercerization applies similar treatments to knitted fabrics, improving their texture and durability. Cloth mercerization focuses on finished cotton fabrics, increasing their luster and resistance to wrinkles and shrinking. Together, these processes elevate the quality and appeal of cotton products across various applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
