<p><strong>The Metallic Luster Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metallic Luster Paint market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic paints contain organic solvents, offering durability and a smooth finish, making them ideal for exterior applications. However, they emit VOCs, raising environmental concerns. In contrast, water-based metallic paints utilize water as a solvent, resulting in lower emission of harmful chemicals and easier clean-up. These paints are gaining popularity for their eco-friendly properties and are suitable for both indoor and outdoor use.</p></p>
