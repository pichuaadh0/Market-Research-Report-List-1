<p><p>The Milk Tofu market features two primary types: Fresh Milk Tofu and Semi-dried Milk Tofu. In contrast, Semi-dried Milk Tofu has undergone a drying process, yielding a firmer consistency and enhanced shelf life. This type is favored for stir-frying and grilling, appealing to consumers seeking convenience and durability in their tofu products.</p></p>
