<p><ul><li>Cotton Spunlace</li><li>Needle-punched Cotton</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonwoven cotton market features types such as cotton spunlace and needle-punched cotton, each serving distinct applications. Cotton spunlace utilizes high-pressure water jets to entangle fibers, creating soft, absorbent fabrics ideal for personal care and medical products. In contrast, needle-punched cotton employs barbed needles to mechanically interlock fibers, resulting in durable materials suited for geotextiles, automotive interiors, and insulation. Both types emphasize versatility and sustainability, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
