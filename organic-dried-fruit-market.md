<p><p>The organic dried fruit market consists of two main types: naturally dried and artificially dried fruits. Naturally dried fruits are produced using methods that retain their natural flavors and nutrients, often employing solar or air drying techniques. In contrast, artificially dried fruits are processed using heat or chemical additives to accelerate drying, which can enhance shelf life and appearance but may reduce nutritional value. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking convenient and nutritious snack options.</p></p>
