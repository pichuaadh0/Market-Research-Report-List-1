<p><p>Point of Purchase (POP) displays are crucial marketing tools in retail environments, categorized into several types. Floor displays are freestanding structures that showcase products prominently on the sales floor. Pallet displays utilize shipping pallets to present bulk products efficiently, often in high-traffic areas. End-cap displays are strategically placed at the end of aisles to attract shopper attention, while countertop displays are smaller units positioned near checkout areas, encouraging impulse purchases. Each type serves to enhance product visibility and drive sales.</p></p>
