<p><p>The Power Hospital Bed market comprises primarily two types: Full Electric Hospital Beds and Semi-Electric Hospital Beds. Full Electric Hospital Beds offer complete electrical control over all functions, including height adjustment, head, and foot positioning, enhancing patient comfort and caregiver convenience. In contrast, Semi-Electric Hospital Beds allow electric adjustment for the head and feet, while manual controls are used for height adjustment, providing a balance between functionality and cost. Both types cater to diverse healthcare settings and patient needs.</p></p>
