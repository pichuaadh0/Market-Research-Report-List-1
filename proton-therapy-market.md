<p><p>The proton therapy market comprises various beam types used for cancer treatment. Proton beam therapy utilizes protons to target tumors with precision, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Electron beam therapy is effective for superficial tumors, delivering treatment to the skin and just beneath it. Neutron beams offer enhanced damage to tumor cells, while carbon ion beams provide higher energy with reduced side effects. Alpha and beta particle beams, though less common, utilize charged particles for specific applications, targeting certain cancers more effectively.</p></p>
