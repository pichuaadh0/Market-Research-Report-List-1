<p><p>The refrigerated display cases market is divided into two main types: chilled and frozen. Chilled display cases maintain temperatures above freezing, typically ranging from 32°F to 54°F, making them ideal for perishable items like dairy, fruits, and vegetables. Frozen display cases operate at temperatures below 0°F, preserving items such as ice cream, frozen meals, and meats. Both types are essential for supermarkets, convenience stores, and restaurants, enhancing product visibility and maintaining freshness to attract consumers.</p></p>
