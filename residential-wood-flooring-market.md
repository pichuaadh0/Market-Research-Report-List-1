<p><strong>The Residential Wood Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-layer Wood Flooring</li><li>Multi-layer Wood Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential wood flooring market is primarily divided into two types: single-layer wood flooring and multi-layer wood flooring. Single-layer wood flooring consists of solid hardwood boards made from a single piece of timber, providing durability and a traditional aesthetic. In contrast, multi-layer wood flooring, often known as engineered wood flooring, features multiple layers of wood fused together, enhancing stability and moisture resistance. This diversity allows consumers to choose flooring that best suits their style preferences and environmental conditions.</p></p>
