<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis Film Market primarily consists of two types: cellulose-based and thin film composite membranes. Cellulose-based membranes, made from natural polymers, are typically less expensive and biodegradable but have lower tolerance to high pressures and temperatures. In contrast, thin film composite membranes offer superior performance with greater filtration efficiency and chemical resistance, making them suitable for a wider range of applications, including desalination and industrial water treatment. Both types play crucial roles in water purification technologies.</p></p>
