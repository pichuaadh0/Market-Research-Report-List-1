<p><ul><li>Optical Surgical Navigation Equipment</li><li>Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical Navigation Equipment market is primarily divided into two types: Optical Surgical Navigation Equipment and Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Equipment. Optical systems use infrared cameras and markers to track instruments, providing high precision and visualization, often employed in spinal and cranial surgeries. In contrast, Electromagnetic systems utilize magnetic fields to determine the position of surgical tools, allowing for rapid setup and versatility in various procedures. Both types enhance surgical accuracy and efficiency, contributing to improved patient outcomes in complex surgeries.</p></p>
