<p><ul><li>Voltage Mode Control</li><li>Current Mode Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switching-mode power supply (SMPS) market primarily features two control types: voltage mode control and current mode control. Voltage mode control regulates output voltage, offering simplicity and stability, making it suitable for applications requiring steady voltage. Current mode control, on the other hand, manages both voltage and current, enhancing response to changes in load conditions and providing better overcurrent protection. Each type caters to distinct needs in industries ranging from consumer electronics to telecommunications, impacting performance and efficiency.</p></p>
