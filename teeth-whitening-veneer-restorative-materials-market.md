<p><p>The teeth whitening veneer restorative materials market primarily comprises two types: resin veneers and porcelain veneers. Resin veneers are made from composite materials, offering a cost-effective and less invasive option for correcting dental imperfections. They can be easily shaped and polished but may be less durable than porcelain. Porcelain veneers, on the other hand, are crafted from ceramic, providing a natural appearance and enhanced strength. They are more stain-resistant and long-lasting, making them a popular choice for achieving an aesthetically pleasing smile.</p></p>
