<p><p>The threaded globe valves market comprises three primary types: cast iron, bronze, and cast steel globe valves. Cast iron globe valves are known for their robustness and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for water and low-pressure applications. Bronze globe valves offer excellent corrosion resistance, particularly in marine environments, while also maintaining strength and durability.
