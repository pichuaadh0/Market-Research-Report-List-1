<p><strong>The Through Beam Laser Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NPN</li><li>PNP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Through Beam Laser Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: NPN and PNP sensors. NPN sensors operate by providing a low (ground) output when activated, suitable for connecting to positive voltage inputs. PNP sensors, conversely, deliver a high (positive) output when triggered, making them ideal for connections to ground. Both types serve distinct applications in automation and safety systems, with the choice depending on the specific electrical configuration required by the equipment they interface with.</p></p>
