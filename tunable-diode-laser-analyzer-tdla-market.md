<p><p>Tunable Diode Laser Analyzers (TDLA) are advanced devices used for precise gas concentration measurements by utilizing tunable diode laser technology. The market for TDLA is segmented into two main types: In-Situ TDLA and Extractive TDLA. In-Situ TDLA measures gas concentrations directly within the process environment, providing real-time data without sample extraction. In contrast, Extractive TDLA involves capturing gas samples and analyzing them externally, offering high sensitivity and accuracy, making it suitable for diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
